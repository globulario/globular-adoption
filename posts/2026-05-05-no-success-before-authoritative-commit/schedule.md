# Posting Schedule — One-week campaign

**Status:** Draft. Nothing is scheduled in any tool.

---

## Schedule table

| Day | Post | Goal | Target audience | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 1 (Mon)** | War story (`post-1-war-story.md`) | Hook the audience with a concrete failure mode. Establish that Globular is a serious distributed system run by people who've felt the pain. | SREs, platform engineers, infra-curious devs | "Has this loop bitten you? What did you do about it?" — invites war-story replies in comments. |
| **Day 2 (Wed)** | Contrarian principle (`post-2-contrarian.md`) | Convert the failure into a stance. Plant the phrase "no success before authoritative commit" in the audience's vocabulary. | Distributed systems crowd, AIOps / agent-tooling builders, technical founders | "Where else does your stack quietly report success before the cluster has accepted it?" — invites self-reflection, not engagement-bait. |
| **Day 3 (Fri)** | Technical mechanism (`post-3-technical.md`) | Earn credibility with engineers who want the actual change. Show the work. | Backend, infra, control-plane engineers | "Diagram and release note in the comments — happy to dig into any of the five PRs." |
| **Day 4 (Sat or Mon)** | Behind-the-scenes / build-in-public *(post not yet drafted)* | Humanize the project. Show the architecture decision came from real operating pain, not a whiteboard. Could be a screenshot of a debugging session, a Slack snippet, or a short reflection on the week. | Builders, indie devs, founders following the journey | "Following along? Reply or DM if you want to test the next release on your own cluster." |
| **Day 5 (following Mon)** | Demo teaser or full demo post (`demo-material.md`) | Convert the principle into evidence. **Conditional on demo being recorded.** | All of the above + skeptics who don't believe distributed-systems posts without proof | "Repo and walkthrough in the comments — tell me what to break next." |

---

## Cadence notes

- Mon → Wed → Fri rhythm respects LinkedIn's mid-week peak without flooding the feed.
- Day 4 (weekend) is intentionally lighter; if traction is low, slide it to the following Monday.
- Day 5 is **conditional** on demo readiness. If the demo isn't recorded by then, defer the slot rather than substitute. A teaser without a payoff hurts more than a missed slot.
- Cross-post Post 2 to X with the shorter version on the same day as the LinkedIn post; Posts 1 and 3 stay LinkedIn-native.
